About Groopy

This application exists because I am a user of the Flickr Views: 25...25000 groups.

The idea of these groups is that as soon as your photos have over a certain number of views they are eligible to be added to the group. However the rules say that a photo can be a member one view group at a time.

I found the process of searching for photos and manually moving them between groups quite laborious.

So I created an application in 2008 called Groopy.
This is the 2016 version

If you like the app or want to comment then please send me a Flickr mail.
